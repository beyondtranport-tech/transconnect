Service Account to Base64 Encoder

Instructions:

  1. Download your service account JSON file from the Firebase Console (Project Settings > Service accounts > Generate new private key).
  2. Open the downloaded JSON file and copy its entire content.
  3. Paste the content into the text area below and click "Encode".
  4. The result is the value for your FIREBASE_ADMIN_SDK_CONFIG_B64 environment variable.

Note: This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your private key is never sent to any server.
